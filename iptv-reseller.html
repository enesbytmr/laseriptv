Reseller FAQ's

How does the IPTV reseller panel work?

When you become a reseller with us, you purchase credits to fill up your reseller panel. The minimum amount is €250. You can expect approximately €20 to equal 1 credit. Depending on the payment method or for larger deposits, larger bonuses are possible, and you'll pay even less per credit, thereby increasing your profits.

How does the IPTV reseller account work?

The reseller account is not time-limited. This means that when you purchase an IPTV reseller account, it will not expire! The only important thing is your credits. You can use credits to create accounts for users. For example, creating a 1-month account costs 0.1 credits. You can continue to sell our service as long as you have credits. If you run out of credits, you can add credits by ordering from us again.
